ABOUT XTIANZ

AI systems intelligence.

XTIANZ is an independent visual operating manual for understanding how AI systems are designed, governed, deployed, financed, and supported by physical infrastructure.

What the site covers

  • AI architecture patterns, agents, workflows, evaluations, and observability.
  • Model Context Protocol, tool security, identity, authorization, and governance.
  • MANGOS companies and their roles across models, distribution, compute, platforms, and connectivity.
  • AI infrastructure, data centers, power, networking, economics, and technology markets.
  • The DMV infrastructure corridor and original local-source navigation.

How content is reviewed

Every long-form guide displays a manual review date. Material protocol, company, regulatory, and market claims are checked against primary sources during review. Significant updates are listed in the article’s review history.

How AI is used

AI may assist research organization, drafting, code, and quality checks. Published content is manually reviewed for clarity, source alignment, and unsupported claims. AI-generated output is not treated as a source.

What XTIANZ is not

The site is not personalized investment, legal, cybersecurity, or engineering advice. Interactive tools produce planning frameworks, not certification or guarantees.
